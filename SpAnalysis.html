Hydrological Riparian Zone
A detailed analysis to derive variable width riparian zones around small lakes in the West Coast and South Coast Natural Resource Regions of BC using LiDAR based high resolution DEM, ArcHydro, and advanced spatial analysis.
Dandelions: Nature's Vibrant Healers
Dandelions are often dismissed as a weed, however this hardy wildflower has much to offer to our ecosystem. This analysis explores the habitats suitable for this plant, countries where they have been native vs. introduced, and lastly crowdsourced observations around the world.
