ArcGIS Survey123 Web Designer


    ArcGIS Survey123 enables the design and distribution of web-based surveys by linking form questions to feature layer fields. Users can customize question behavior, share surveys via links or QR codes, and store responses directly in the feature layer for seamless access and analysis. For quick survey creation, Web Designer, a browser-based tool on the Survey123 website, provides a drag-and-drop interface for effortlessly building surveys from scratch or templates.
